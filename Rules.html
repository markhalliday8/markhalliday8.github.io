Rules

Scoring Rules
You are scored based on lap speed. The fastest lap wins.You earn one point per player you beat. For example, finishing first in a race of six rewards six points. There is an individual scoreboard and team scoreboard.
Team Scoring
Each player's points combined equates to a team's score.
Uneven Teams
If someone drops out or a team has an uneven number, the team with more players will not have their additional players points counted. For example: Team one has 2 players and team two has three players. The lowest scoring player of team two will not count each week.
